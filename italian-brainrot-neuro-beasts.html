Core Gameplay: Click, Collect, and Curate Your Crypto-Creatures!

The essence of Neuro Beasts is beautifully simple yet offers layers of engaging progression:

  • Click to Gather Resources: Your primary activity involves clicking or tapping anywhere on the screen, particularly on your active Neuro Beast. Each interaction rewards you with valuable Neuro-Chips and delicious Pizzas.
  • Accumulate and Enhance: Hoard your Neuro-Chips and Pizzas to unlock a vast array of peculiar Neuro Beasts and powerful upgrades. These enhancements are not just numerical boosts; they tangibly improve your characters and resource-generating abilities.
  • Automate the Absurdity: Invest in upgrades and systems that passively generate Neuro-Chips and Pizzas, even when you're not actively clicking. Imagine your "Bombardiro Crocodilo" contributing to your cause while you're offline!
  • Witness Your Menagerie Evolve: As your production of Neuro-Chips and Pizzas skyrockets, you'll unlock new Neuro Beasts, each more bizarre than the last. The game world may even transform to reflect your growing collection of AI-generated wonders.
